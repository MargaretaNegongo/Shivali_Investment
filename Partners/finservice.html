Trade Finance

SIC & MA provides a comprehensive approach to structuring complex trade transactions for a variety of stakeholders, including importers, exporters, and trading companies. SIC & MA experienced team understands that providing trade finance in today’s volatile global markets demands creativity and flexibility. As such, SIC & MA utilizes an array of trade and export finance instruments to mitigate unnecessary credit risks while increasing clients’ access to working capital. MA provides specialized supply chain finance facilities, tailored to the specifics of each deal. SIC & MA general services typically fall into one of the following four (5) categories: 1. Documentary Letters of Credit, 2. Standby Letters of Credit, 3. letter of Credit, 4. Guarantees and 5. SWIFT messaging. In keeping with MA mission statement, MA team tailors each instrument to meet the requirements of each transaction.

We at Shivali Investments and Consulting (SIC) are therefore able to provide you with the following services:

Trade Financing

Trade finance makes it possible and easier for importers and exporters to transact business through trade.

Project Financing

Project finance is the funding (financing) of long-term infrastructure, industrial projects, and public services using a non-recourse or limited recourse financial structure.

Private Equity

Private equity is an alternative investment class and consists of capital that is not listed on a public exchange.

Debt Syndication

Debt syndication involves a group of lenders funding various portions of a loan to a single borrower. A syndicated loan is a structured product that needs to be arranged and administered effectively.

Structured Finance

Structured finance is a heavily involved financial instrument presented to large financial institutions or companies with complicated financing needs who are unsatisfied with conventional financial products.
