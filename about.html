Who Are We?

Over 17 years experience in the financial industry of which we have 8 years of experience in the auditing industry where work was primarily aimed at providing an efficient and proactive service to clients to ensure that their needs are fully met. During this period we worked on clients in various industries including freight and service companies, hospitality, mining, education and farming.

We have 1 year experience as management accountant in a large cement factory. Work was aimed at preparing management accounts and comparing figures according to monthly deadlines, providing assistance to managers for the preparation of their budgets, scrutinizing information and identifying problem areas and suggesting solutions to top level management and providing support assistance during the audit.
